Privacy Policy

At Local Services Hub, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, phone number when you register or book a service.
  • Service usage data, including services viewed, booking history, and preferences.
  • Payment information when applicable, though sensitive payment details are processed securely by our partners.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, manage, and improve our services.
  • To communicate important updates, confirmations, and promotions.
  • To analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience.
  • To comply with legal requirements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Trusted service providers to complete your booking.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and tracking technologies to improve functionality, analyze usage, and provide personalized content. You can manage cookies via your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We adopt appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. User Rights

  • You can request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal information.
  • You can unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage users to read their privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

9. Contact Us

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact Page.
