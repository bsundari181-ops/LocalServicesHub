Refund & Cancellation Policy

Local Services Hub values your trust and satisfaction. This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines how cancellations and refunds are handled for booked services.

1. Cancellation by Users

  • Users can cancel bookings before the service starts by contacting the service provider or using our platform if supported.
  • Cancellations should be made at least 24 hours in advance to be eligible for a full refund, unless specified otherwise.
  • Late cancellations or no-shows may result in partial or no refund depending on the service provider's terms.

2. Cancellation by Service Providers

  • If a service provider cancels, users will receive a full refund for prepaid services.
  • We ensure that alternative providers are offered wherever possible.

3. Refund Process

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after approval.
  • Refund amounts may vary based on cancellation timing, service type, or third-party charges.

4. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may apply if a service has already been partially delivered or if additional costs have been incurred.

5. How to Request a Refund

  • Contact the service provider directly via WhatsApp or the platform messaging system.
  • Provide booking details and reason for cancellation/refund.
  • Await confirmation from the provider or our support team.

6. Modifications to Bookings

Users can request to reschedule or modify a booking. Rescheduling policies depend on the service provider and may affect refund eligibility.

7. Disputes

In case of disputes regarding cancellations or refunds, Local Services Hub may mediate between the user and service provider to reach a fair resolution.

8. Contact Us

For questions regarding our Refund & Cancellation Policy, please contact us via the Contact Page.
